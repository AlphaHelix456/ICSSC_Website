ICSSC @ UCI


Information and Computer Sciences Student Council
We are the official student government for the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences.

About Us

Our Mission


To improve student life of the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences academically, socially, and professionally by creating and deepening the bonds between students, faculty, administration, companies, and alumni of the ICS School as well as similar groups outside of the Information of Computer Science

Corporate Outreach Committee

  • Help connect technical recruiters with ICS School talent
  • Coordinate info sessions with local companies
  • Network with hiring managers and ICS Alumni
  • Help students get internships and jobs

Projects Committee

  • Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more
  • Apply new skills
  • Collaborate together
  • Create cool projects

Student Events Committee

  • Plan and host social events
  • Market events on various platforms
  • Handle event logistics
  • Prepare for ICS Day

ICS Day

Check out the website

ICS Day is a carnival-like celebration of everything Computer Science, bringing together students, faculty, and staff from all over UC Irvine. ICS Day displays the unique talents and culture that makes up our community and is our day to show the world that we are so much more than meets the eye. More details will come next quarter!
